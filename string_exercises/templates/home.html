Welcome to Jumble!

Instructions

Play

  1. Hit "Create Puzzle"
  2. Enter the Question (The clue)
  3. Enter the Answer (The final scrambled word)
  4. Select a Difficulty (Affects the length of each word to unscramble)
  5. Share the link for the puzzle!

Add Words

  1. You can add custom words to the dictionary to pick from
  2. Currently, the puzzle picks from a collection of nearly 2k generic words
  3. Always specify the Category of the words being added
  4. You can add words from CSV file that contains a column of words with the column title "Word" (Exact Match)
  5. On the next release, you can select the category of words to pick your puzzle from
